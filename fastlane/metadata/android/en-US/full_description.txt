<p>Block unknown callers using a call screening service.</p>
<p>By default block phone numbers that are not saved in your contacts list.</p>
Optionally:
<ul>
  <li>allow numbers that you have called</li>
  <li>allow numbers with toll-free codes (ex: 800)</li>
  <li>allow a third call from the same number within five minutes (cache constraint)</li>
  <li>allow temporary mobile numbers from incoming text messages</li>
  <li>allow numbers that passed STIR verification (android 11)</li>
</ul>
